Frequently Asked Questions

How is the $2,000 minimum possible? expand_more

We use a fractional ownership model that allows multiple investors to pool resources into a single franchise unit. This lowers the entry barrier while maintaining the same proportional ROI as a sole owner.

What is the average ROI for your franchises? expand_more

While ROI varies by sector, our curated portfolio typically aims for 12-18% annual returns. Detailed financial projections are available for each verified investor.

Can I sell my stake later? expand_more

Yes, we have a secondary market where investors can list their shares for sale to other verified users on the e-Franquicias platform after an initial lock-in period.
